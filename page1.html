Geomatics deals with any technology and methods for collecting, managing, processing and disribution of spatial data. From high precision construction surveys to the GNSS positioning in your smart phone, geomatics concepts are used globally everyday!

Here's a quick list of some applications of geomatics:
  • Hyrodgraphic and Terrestrial surveys
  • Geodesy
  • Remote Sensing and Photogrammetry
  • Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS)
  • Geographic Infomation Science
